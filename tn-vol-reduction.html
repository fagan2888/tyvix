Diversification

Diversification is arguably the most foundational volatility reduction technique adopted by investors. One of the core challenges of reducing volatility through diversification is that the relationship between returns on different assets is not constant over time and may break down just when markets start to become volatile; the global financial crisis of 2007-2008 highlighted this danger when correlations across many assets spiked dramatically.
